# Product Overview

Maple Quest is a location-based social application that gamifies exploration and discovery. Users can visit physical locations, earn points, collect achievements, and share their experiences with friends through photos and notes.

## Core Features

- **Location Discovery**: Users visit real-world locations to earn points and unlock achievements
- **Social Networking**: Friend system with friend requests, acceptance/rejection workflow
- **Photo Sharing**: Users can upload and share images from their visits with descriptions and likes
- **Gamification**: Points-based system with achievements to encourage exploration
- **User Profiles**: Custom user profiles with profile pictures and activity tracking

## Target Use Case

The app encourages users to explore their surroundings, discover new places, and share experiences with their social network while earning rewards for their adventures.
